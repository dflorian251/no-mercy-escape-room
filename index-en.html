KARMA

Karma, an uncontrollable force of the universe, a terrifying judge of our actions. With every right or wrong action, we affect every second of our lives. It is a mirror. Every time we do something, we leave a reflection on it forever. And when we later look again, we see the consequences of our actions. Some people believe in it while others don't. But those who believe know that no secret remains hidden. No crime is omitted. No act goes unpunished. When the karma begins to unravel, nothing can change, or can it ?
